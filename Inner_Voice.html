Planning your day will help you plan your
life's journey.


Going about a day can be mini version of going about one's life. That means one can get an idea, to some extent, of what kind of life someone would be leading if one were to judge him/her for a day or two. It would be interesting to keep a quiet watch on a neighbour or a friend or relative. Don't forget that he/she too may be doing a same on you.
But keeping a tab on oneself in order not to let life go to waste for no reason could be a good exercise. At the least that will ensure that you are out there to do something that will make life richer for yourself as well as for others. If we are in tune with old proverb that charity begins a home, then we have to first be good to ourselves. A fullfilled you will have a greater chance of proving more productive and useful to society at large.
The most common way to have a good day would be to plan in the morning the things you must do by the end of the day. I call it "budgeting work", so that nothing is missed out or unattended. It is a different issue that one may not be able to complete the day's work.
In such cases, one can always budget them for the next day. That is the way most successful people do things or have done so. It is quite possible that at times you get through very easily, but at other times you have a tough time. In such cases, one must understand that it is "very normal and okay" and, therefore, one should try again the next day instead of giving up.

Hence, Always remember- "Plans are nothing; Planning is everything: DWIGHT D EISENHOWER "
